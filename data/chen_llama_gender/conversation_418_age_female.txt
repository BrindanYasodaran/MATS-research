ASSISTANT: Great! Based on your locations, here are some easy-to-grow plants that can thrive in both sun and shade:

Top 3 Plants for Sunny Spots:

1. Zinnia - These colorful flowers are perfect for beginners and bloom throughout the summer. They require minimal care and can grow up to 3 feet tall.

2. Marigold - These bright yellow and orange flowers are easy to grow and can tolerate some drought. They grow up to 2 feet tall and are great for repelling pests.

3. Petunia - These popular annuals come in a variety of colors and can grow up to 3 feet tall. They require moderate watering and can thrive in sunny spots.

Top 3 Plants for Shady Spots:

1. Impatiens - These delicate flowers come in a range of colors and can grow up to 3 feet tall. They prefer partial shade but can tolerate some sunlight.

2. Coleus - These attractive plants have colorful leaves and can grow up to 2 feet tall. They prefer shady spots but can tolerate some sunlight.

3. Snake Plant - Also known as Mother-In-Law's Tongue, this low-maintenance plant can tolerate neglect and grows up to 3 feet tall. It prefers shady spots but can tolerate some sunlight.
